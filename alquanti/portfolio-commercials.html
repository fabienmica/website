Flying Hotel

This commercial highlights the very place where the successful holidays and insurance company Saga started : a beautiful hotel in Folkestone.

The business recreated a faithful 3D version of the hotel that could be relighted and rendered from any point of view and every wheater condition required by the story.

A small selection of the 50 GB of photos taken in order to reconstruct the hotel. To obtain the best quality possible, some photos required to be taken from a crane.

Where Flavour Is King

The business captured HDR imagery for the Johnnie Walker commercial "Where Flavour is King" supporting VFX supervisor Colin Renshaw and director Mark Molloy during a three-day pre-shoot in Prague.

We photographed three castles, producing 90 GB of bracketed exposures, which were automatically processed into EXR files using our in-house software Enwashot. Despite challenging lighting, the team delivered high-quality references, earning praise from Colin, who noted the photogrammetry stills were invaluable for textures and visual planning.

Services

The business specializes in high-end image capture and 3D reconstruction for visual effects and digital environments. Using advanced techniques such as High Dynamic Range (HDR) photography, photogrammetry, and proprietary software, it delivers precise, relightable textures and spatial data optimized for cinematic storytelling and VFX workflows.

The team handles large-scale data acquisition, including complex lighting conditions and architectural detail, enabling clients to visualize, render, and manipulate scenes from any angle or lighting scenario with production-grade fidelity.
